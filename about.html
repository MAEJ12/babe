About Us

Meet the team behind the Choose Life Campaign.

Mrs.Rheena Mae Montes

Impowerment Technology Adviser

Rheena Mae Montes is a dedicated educator and mentor, currently teaching at Rosemont Hills Montessori College. With a passion for guiding young minds, she plays a crucial role in shaping the direction of the Choose Life Campaign. As the adviser, she ensures that the campaign remains impactful, well-structured, and aligned with its core mission of promoting a drug-free lifestyle. Her experience in education and counseling allows her to provide insightful guidance, helping the team develop meaningful initiatives that truly resonate with the community. Through her leadership, the campaign fosters awareness, encourages open discussions about addiction prevention, and empowers individuals to make informed, life-changing decisions. Rheena’s unwavering commitment to advocacy and education makes her an invaluable force behind the campaign’s success.

James Sanchez

Group Leader

James Sanchez: A Passionate Leader

James Sanchez is a passionate leader and a dedicated student at Rosemont Hills Montessori College. As the Group Leader of the Choose Life Campaign, he plays a vital role in spearheading initiatives that promote awareness and education about the dangers of drug abuse.

Commitment to the Campaign

With a strong sense of responsibility and commitment, James ensures that the campaign's vision is effectively carried out. He coordinates with team members, organizes outreach programs, and actively engages with peers to create a supportive and informed community.

Inspiring Others

His leadership is not just about directing efforts—it’s about inspiring others to take a stand, make informed choices, and advocate for a healthier, drug-free future.

Connecting with Peers

As a student himself, James understands the challenges young individuals face. His perspective allows him to connect with others on a personal level, making the Choose Life Campaign not just an initiative, but a movement driven by the youth, for the youth.

A Key Pillar of Success

His determination and leadership make him a key pillar of the campaign’s success.
